    RareBirds 1st 2019 PH Summit

    Theo January 28, 2019 events

    Hi! Today, we are having our 1st PH summit for 2019, how exciting is it???

    To have an awesome kick-off this year and promote collaboration, the PH team decided to have our first 3-day summit! 

    The venue for this event is here in Sharvd Coworking Space, Makati Philippines which has a nice place to collaborate. 

    Day one

    Everyone from all over the philippines have gathered here in makati to be part of the awesome event. The early part of the day is mostly travelling! Most of us have arrived here in the venue, early which is awesome.

    These are the list of activities we have in line for the day
    • Catching-up! (Story telling, listening to weird laughs, making the mood light)
    • 360 Degree Evaluations
    • Presentation of the organizational chart
    • Sprint Retrospective
    • Visa Application
    Catching-up while doing 360 degree evaluations!

    This is almost all of the dev team! They are busy multi-tasking (evaluating & story-telling)

     

    I will be sending more updates this afternoon!
